Clear privacy, so you can use VeVolt with confidence.

VeVolt is designed to provide useful information through clear, purpose-specific permissions.

Personal data stays on-device

Vehicle details, saved locations and personal history remain in Android private storage.

Permissions under your control

Location and camera access are used only for map, route and QR Code features.

Google Play payments

Premium features are processed securely through your Google Play account.

Community controls

Reports can be flagged, blocked and deleted by their author; photos are processed to strip metadata.
