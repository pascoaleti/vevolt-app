# Third-party fonts

VeVolt bundles the following font files from the official Google Fonts repository:

- Sora variable font: `app/src/main/res/font/sora_variable.ttf`
- Inter variable font: `app/src/main/res/font/inter_variable.ttf`

Both families are distributed under the SIL Open Font License 1.1. Sources and
license texts are available at:

- https://github.com/google/fonts/tree/main/ofl/sora
- https://github.com/google/fonts/tree/main/ofl/inter
